What makes a good place to stay at?

A good stay is made up for four different factors: i) comfort ii) aesthetics iii) service iv) coexistence with it's surroundings. Let's start with the first one: comfort. What creates comfort: a place that feels warm, no noise, good beds, darkness, good temperature.
